# Product Guide: TEDxBhrikutiMandap 2026

## Initial Concept
Build a web application for the TEDxBhrikutiMandap 2026 event.

## Primary Objective: Event Awareness
The core goal of the TEDxBhrikutiMandap 2026 website is to showcase the upcoming event, create awareness, and build excitement among potential attendees and sponsors.

## Current Focus: Premium "Coming Soon" Page
For the initial launch, the project will focus on a high-quality, elegant, and premium "Coming Soon" landing page. This page will serve as the digital face of the event before the full feature set (Speaker Lineup, Event Schedule, Contact Forms) is integrated.

## Target Audience
- **Potential Attendees:** Individuals in the local and global community who are interested in TEDx talks and the 2026 theme.
- **Sponsors/Partners:** Organizations looking to support the event and gain visibility.

## Key Features (Future Phases)
- **Speaker Lineup:** Detailed profiles of featured speakers and their talk titles.
- **Event Schedule:** A comprehensive timeline of sessions and activities.
- **Contact/Inquiry Form:** A dedicated channel for general inquiries and sponsorship requests.

## Constraints & Requirements
- **Brand Compliance:** All visual and written content must strictly adhere to official TEDx branding guidelines.
- **Performance & Accessibility:** The website must be fast-loading and provide a seamless experience for all users, regardless of device or ability.
- **Aesthetic:** Elegant, premium, and sophisticated visual design.
